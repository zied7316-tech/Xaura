═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
  SIMPLE FIX FOR WHATSAPP ERROR - STEP BY STEP
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════

PROBLEM:
Your WhatsApp number is wrong. Twilio doesn't recognize it.

SOLUTION:
Replace the wrong number with the correct one from Twilio.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
STEP 1: FIND YOUR WHATSAPP NUMBER
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Go to: https://console.twilio.com/
2. Click "Messaging" (left menu)
3. Click "Try it out"
4. Click "Send a WhatsApp message"
5. You'll see a number like: +1 415 523 8886
6. Write this number down!

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
STEP 2: JOIN SANDBOX (DO THIS ONCE)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. On the same page, find the "join" code
2. Open WhatsApp on your phone
3. Send: join <code> to the number from Step 1
4. Wait for confirmation
5. Done!

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
STEP 3: UPDATE RAILWAY
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Go to: https://railway.app/dashboard
2. Click your project
3. Click "Variables" tab
4. Find: TWILIO_WHATSAPP_NUMBER
5. Change value to: whatsapp:+14155238886
   (Replace +14155238886 with YOUR number from Step 1)
   (Remove spaces, add whatsapp: at start)
6. Save
7. Go to "Deployments" tab
8. Click "Redeploy"
9. Wait 2-3 minutes

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
STEP 4: TEST
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Book an appointment
2. Check WhatsApp - you should get a message!

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════

RIGHT: whatsapp:+14155238886  ✅

The number MUST come from Twilio Console!
